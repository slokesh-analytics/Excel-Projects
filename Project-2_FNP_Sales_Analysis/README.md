# FNP Sales Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project involves a comprehensive sales analysis for **Ferns and Petals (FNP)**, a leading gifting platform. The objective was to analyze a dataset of 1,000 orders from 2023 to uncover actionable insights regarding seasonal sales trends, customer spending behavior, and product performance across various occasions like Diwali, Holi, and Valentine's Day.

## 📊 Business Questions Addressed
The analysis focuses on answering 10 key business questions, including:
* What is the total revenue and average customer spending?
* Which months and occasions (e.g., Raksha Bandhan, Valentine's Day) drive the highest sales?
* How does order quantity impact delivery time?
* Identifying the top 5 products by revenue and the top 10 cities by order volume.

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
* **Microsoft Excel**: Primary tool for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization.
* **Data Cleaning**: Handled messy datasets, managed date/time formatting, and used nested `IF` statements.
* **Pivot Tables**: Used to aggregate data for monthly performance and product popularity.
* **Dashboards**: Created an interactive visual summary to track KPIs like Total Revenue (₹3.52M) and Average Delivery Time (5.5 days).

## 📈 Key Insights
* **Revenue Peak**: February and August emerged as the highest-grossing months, likely driven by Valentine's Day and Raksha Bandhan.
* **Top Performer**: The 'Colors' category was the top revenue generator, contributing over ₹1M to total sales.
* **Efficiency**: The average delivery time across all categories is approximately 5.5 days, with 'Plants' having the fastest turnaround.
* **Geographic Reach**: High order volumes were identified in cities like Imphal and Dhanbad, suggesting strong regional market penetration.
